About MiniSo

MiniSo is a multi-page front-end e-commerce demo featuring product browsing, a responsive shopping cart, and simple authentication via localStorage. Designed with a clean, minimalist UI, it showcases skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for creating engaging online shopping experiences.
